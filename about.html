About Us

Welcome to Dignan’s, we’re a catering company that has been serving the local businesses in and around Edinburgh for the past five years. We combine our passion for good food with excellent customer service. Our aim is to provide our clients with a unique culinary experience that is both memorable and delicious.

We use the freshest ingredients, sourced locally to create our dishes. With our team made up of dedicated professionals, we are committed to delivering the highest quality of service to our clients. Whether you are hosting a corporate event, a private party, or a wedding, we have the expertise and experience to make your occasion a success. Thank you for choosing Dignan’s, we look forward to serving you soon.

Dignans started small, with just five passionate individuals committed to delivering the best catering experience in Edinburgh. Over the past five years, we have grown to a team of over 30 professionals, all sharing our passion for food and customer service. Our growth is a testament to our commitment to excellence and our clients' satisfaction. We are proud of what we have achieved so far, but we are not stopping there. We are still expanding, exploring new opportunities to serve our clients better and reach new markets. At Dignans, we believe that the sky is the limit, and we are always pushing ourselves to go beyond our boundaries.
